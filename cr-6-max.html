CR-6 Max

Printer Details

The CR-6 Max is a versatile 3D printer known for its large build volume and high-quality prints. It is capable of printing with a variety of materials, including PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU, and more. The CR-6 Max is a great choice for printing large, functional parts.

The CR-6 Max is a Cartesian-style printer, meaning that it uses a gantry system to move the print head along the X and Y axes, and a bed that moves along the Z axis. The CR-6 Max is equipped with a heated bed, which allows it to print with a variety of materials. The CR-6 Max is also equipped with a filament runout sensor, which pauses the print if the printer runs out of filament.

Specifications

  • Printing Technology: FDM
  • Build Volume: 400mm x 400mm x 400mm
  • Layer Resolution: 0.1mm - 0.4mm
  • Maximum Print Speed: 80mm/s
  • Maximum Travel Speed: 150mm/s
  • Maximum Hotend Temperature: 260°C
  • Maximum Heated Bed Temperature: 110°C
  • Supported Materials: PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU, and more
  • Connectivity: USB, SD Card
  • Printer Dimensions: 650mm x 550mm x 720mm
  • Printer Weight: 15kg
