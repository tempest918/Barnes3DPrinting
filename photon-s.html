Anycubic Photon S

Printer Details

The Anycubic Photon S is a high-resolution resin-based 3D printer that uses a UV light source to cure liquid resin into solid objects. The Photon S is capable of printing at a layer height of 0.01mm, which is 1/10th the thickness of a sheet of paper. This allows for extremely high-resolution prints with an exceptional surface finish. The Photon S is capable of printing at a maximum build volume of 115mm x 65mm x 165mm.

The Photon S is capable of printing with a wide variety of resins, including standard resins, flexible resins, and castable resins. The Photon S is also capable of printing with third-party resins, which allows for a wide variety of colors and material properties.

Specifications

  • Printing Technology: Resin-based (SLA/DLP)
  • Build Volume: 115mm x 65mm x 165mm
  • Layer Height: 0.01mm - 0.2mm
  • XY Resolution: 0.047mm
  • Light Source: UV LED
  • Connectivity: USB
  • Printer Dimensions: 230mm x 200mm x 400mm
  • Printer Weight: 4.5kg
