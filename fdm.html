FDM Printing Service

Service Overview

Our FDM Printing Service offers high-quality printing using advanced FDM technology. We specialize in creating detailed prototypes, functional parts, and models.

The prints will be completed on the Creality CR-6 Max, which is a large-scale printer that can print objects up to 400mm x 400mm x 400mm in size. The CR-6 Max is capable of printing with a wide range of materials, including PLA, PETG, ABS, and more.

For examples of completed prints check out the CR-6 Max Prints page.

Key Features

  • High precision and accuracy
  • Wide range of material options
  • Quick turnaround time
  • Ability to print complex geometries
  • Durable and functional prototypes
  • Support for large-scale printing
