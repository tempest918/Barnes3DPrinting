SLA Printing Service

Service Overview

Our SLA Printing Service provides high-resolution prints with an exceptional surface finish. SLA printing is ideal for small, detailed prints that require a high level of accuracy. SLA printing is also ideal for printing models that require a high level of detail, such as figurines, miniatures, and jewelry.

Our SLA printer is the Anycubic Photon S. The Photon S is a DLP printer that uses a UV light source to cure liquid resin. The Photon S has a build volume of 115 x 65 x 165 mm and a layer resolution of 0.01 mm.

For examples of completed prints check out the Photon S Prints page.
