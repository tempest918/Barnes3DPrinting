BARNES 3D PRINTING

My name is Anthony Barnes and I am a 3D printing enthusiast. I have been 3D printing for several years now and have decided to start my own 3D printing business. I have two 3D printers, the CR-6 Max and the Anycubic Photon S. I offer both FDM and SLA printing services. I also have a gallery/portfolio of some of my prints. If you are interested in my services, please contact me.
